# Graitec Group

Graitec Group is an international software company developing BIM, CAD, and engineering solutions for the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) industry.

Our technologies support structural engineers, designers, and construction professionals in designing, analyzing, and managing building projects through advanced digital workflows, simulation tools, and automation.

## What you will find here

This GitHub organization hosts projects related to:
- engineering and structural analysis technologies
- BIM and CAD integrations
- cloud services and APIs

## About Graitec

Graitec helps organizations accelerate digital transformation in construction and manufacturing by combining industry expertise with advanced software technologies.
